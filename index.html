Bio Sketch

I am currently a Professor at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. I received my Ph.D. degree on August 2011 under the supervision of Professor K. J. Ray Liu. Before that, I got my Bachelor degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2004 and Master degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2007.

Research interests

Signal Processing, Wireless Communications, Information Security, Multimedia, Data Science, Network Science, Game Theory
