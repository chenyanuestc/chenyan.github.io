Bio Sketch

I have been a Professor at University of Science and Technology of China since March 2020. I was a Professor at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China from Sept. 2015 to Feb. 2020. I received my Ph.D. degree from University of Maryland College Park on August 2011. Before that, I got my Bachelor degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2004 and Master degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2007.

Research interests

Multimedia, Radio Vision (radio-image multimodal sensing), Wireless Communications
